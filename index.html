Research

Artistic practice

At the heart of our work is research into technology as a catalyst for emotions. We use digital media to amplify experiences, creating lasting impressions on those who engage with them.

We explore human-machine interactions where the digital fades into the background, emphasizing relationships, the tangible, listening, the everyday, the pause, observation… the human.
Through our work, we invite reflection, curiosity, and deeper engagement with technology, art, and multidisciplinary creation.
Orbiting around our practice, a constellation of proposals emerges—spaces for knowledge exchange, encounters with other artists, and collaborations that expand our perspectives. We share what inspires and challenges us, what questions and unsettles us.
